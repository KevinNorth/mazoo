Mazoo is a maze game. The goal is to traverse as many mazes as you can, accumulating as many points as possible along the way. You have sixty seconds in which to accomplish this task.

Along the way, you'll encounter yellow stars and blue stars. The first yellow star in each level gives you one extra point. The second gives you four points. The blue star will add an extra ten seconds to your time limit.

Finishing a maze gives you five points.

How to Play

You can either use the mouse, your finger (if on a touchscreen), or the keyboard.

If using the mouse or touchscreen, simply drag the cursor through the maze. You can also tap any highlighted square to be moved there. (This is particularly handy on the 3D and 4D levels, to navigate the stairs/portals.)

If using the keyboard, wasd is your friend. The w and s keys move you (respectively) up and down, and a and d move you left and right. On the 3D and 4D levels, i and k move you vertically between levels, and j and k move you horizontally. (The ijkl keys will only work when you are positioned on one of the red pointers.)

If that's all you needed to know, go ahead and return to the menu!

How does this thing work?

The mazes are generated using the Growing Tree algorithm, choosing the last cell 75% of the time, and the most heavily weighted cell the other 25% of the time.

If you're curious about the implementation, the source code for Mazoo! is available on GitHub, here:

https://github.com/jamis/mazoo
