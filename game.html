You have five minutes to traverse as many mazes as you possibly can. At the end of each maze, you'll immediately be taken to the next (slightly harder) random maze.

Pick up yellow stars for points (1 point for the first in each stage, 4 points for the second). Pick up the blue star for an extra 10 seconds on the clock.

Completing each maze will give you 5 points.

How far can you go? How many points can you earn?
